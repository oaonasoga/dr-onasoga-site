About

Dr. Onasoga, Olukayode A., holds a PhD in Computer Science from Universiti Utara Malaysia (UUM), Malaysia, specialising in AI-driven solutions, particularly focusing on deep learning, fuzzy inference systems, multimodal sentiment analysis, and AI explainability—a critical aspect of developing transparent AI systems. His academic journey includes a Master’s degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Covenant University, Ogun State, Nigeria, and a Bachelor’s in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ogun State, Nigeria. Olukayode’s technical proficiency encompasses programming languages and tools essential for AI and data science, such as Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Keras, alongside a profound understanding of data visualisation techniques. Additionally, he has contributed to AI research, publishing papers on fuzzy logic integration and neural networks. Olukayode is a dynamic and results-driven academic, researcher, and educator with progressive experience and expertise in Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Machine Learning, and Data Science. He demonstrates expertise in curriculum design, interdisciplinary research, and student-centred pedagogy across undergraduate and postgraduate levels. Olukayode boasts a strong record of peer-reviewed publications, successful grant acquisition, and the supervision of MSc and PhD students, which has led to impactful academic and industry outcomes. He is adept at integrating innovative AI techniques in healthcare, finance, education, and policy domains. As a strategic collaborator in international research networks and partnerships with industry and government agencies, Olukayode is passionate about fostering inclusive learning environments and contributing to institutional growth through teaching, research, and academic service.
